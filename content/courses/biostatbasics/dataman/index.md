## Learning outcomes
In this section, you will learn how to make
- good folder structure for your project
- good documentation
- Readme.txt
- habits on coding.

Learning data management can help you
- ensure the analysis is reproducible
- work coherently and efficiently with yourself 
- ensure the project can be understood by others (supervisors, collaborators, and future readers)
- create a good work flow  
- enhance accuracy of work.
