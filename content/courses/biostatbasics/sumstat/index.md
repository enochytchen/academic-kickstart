## Learning outcomes
In this section, you will learn how to
-  get to know the data by performing ```summarize```, ```describe```, ```codebook```, ```list```
- measure central tendency of the data (mean, median, amd mode)
- measure dispersion of the data (range, interquartile range, variance, and standard deviation)
- make a two-by-two table ad interpret relative risk and odds ratio from it by using ```csi``` in ```epitab``` package
